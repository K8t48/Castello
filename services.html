Accessibility Services

At Castello we offer a wide range of services to help you meet your accessibility needs. Our team is trained and certified to ensure your digtial media meets Section 508, ADA and WCAG standards.

Audit

Let us do a detailed accessibility audit. We will evaluate your entire website and provide a detailed report of how to improve your site to meet the required accessibility guidelines.

Document Accessibility

We can make all of your documents accessibile meeting WCAG standards. Let us teach your team to create accessibile documents so everyone of any ability can access all important information.

Accessibility Remediation

Once we have completed the accessibility let us work together to fix the accessibility errors so we insure your website is fully compliant.

Design Solutions

Are you creating a new website? Accessibilty planning should begin from day one. We can create wireframes and mockups or we can work with your design team to advise on best accessibility practices.

Usability Testing

It is important to test the designs with potential customers, this includes testing designs with users with disabilities who use assistive technology. This way we can ensure your website not only meets WCAG guidelines but is also usable and intuitive for everyone.

Accessibility Training

Let us teach your team best accessibility practices. We can customize our lessons to meet your teams individual needs.
