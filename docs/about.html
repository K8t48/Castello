Hi I am Katie!

Certified Section 508 Certified Tester and WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance Specialist

As websites have become more advanced and technology has evolved accessibility is often left as an afterthought. In the rush to keep up many companies have made the choice to not prioritize digital accessibility leaving 26% of the United States population who have a disability unable to fully access digital information. This is one of the many reasons I created Castello Consultation.

At Castello Consultation we are a small team passionate about making the web and digital media accessibile for everyone. We are excited to work wtih you and help make your digtial media meet and exceed WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and Section 508 compliance.
